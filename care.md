Fixail Care – Brand-Centric After-Sales Service ERP & Field Service Management System

A centralized platform designed to manage end-to-end after-sales operations for brands, including service orders, technician workflows, parts inventory, warranty logic, and payment orchestration.

Built to transform fragmented service operations into a structured, trackable, and scalable enterprise system

🧠 Problem Statement

After-sales service operations for brands are highly chaotic and inefficient:

No centralized system to manage:
Service requests
Technician assignments
Spare parts flow
Warranty handling is complex and error-prone
Payments split between brand and customer
Lack of visibility into:
Technician performance
Inventory movement
Service history
Heavy dependency on manual coordination

👉 Result:

Delays, poor tracking, financial inconsistencies, and operational inefficiency
