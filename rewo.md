REWOSTORE – CONSTRUCTION MATERIAL ECOSYSTEM PLATFORM
🔥 Hero Section

Rewostore – Multi-Role Construction Material Marketplace with Integrated Logistics System

A scalable platform connecting contractors, suppliers, and transporters with real-time order flow, logistics coordination, and multi-role approval workflows.

Designed to digitize and streamline construction material procurement with end-to-end visibility and control.

🧠 Problem Statement

Construction material procurement is highly unstructured and inefficient:

Contractors depend on manual calls & local vendors
No transparency in pricing, supplier availability, or delivery timelines
Logistics coordination between supplier & transporter is fragmented
No centralized system for:
Order tracking
Worksite-based management
Payment visibility
High delays and miscommunication across stakeholders

👉 Result:

Operational inefficiency, delays, and cost unpredictability

💡 Solution Overview

Rewostore introduces a multi-role transactional ecosystem:
