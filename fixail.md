Fixail – Multi-Service Super App with Modular Micro-Frontend Architecture

A scalable ecosystem combining on-demand services, spare parts marketplace, quick services, and B2B maintenance, powered by a unified backend and modular app architecture.

Designed as a future-ready platform capable of scaling across countries, services, and business models without rebuilding the core system

🧠 Problem Statement

Modern service platforms fail due to fragmentation and scalability limitations:

Services, spare parts, and business tools exist as separate disconnected systems
Hard to scale across multiple cities / countries
No unified workflow between customer, technician, and admin
Real-time operations like tracking, dispatch, and pricing become complex
Difficulty in adding new service domains without breaking existing systems

👉 Businesses either:

Build multiple apps → high cost & complexity
Or build one monolith → impossible to scale
💡 Solution Overview

Fixail solves this by building a modular super app ecosystem:

🧩 Core Strategy
Single Customer App (Main App)
Features delivered as modular packages (micro frontends):
Core Services
Spares Marketplace
Quick Services
Business (B2B SaaS)
Separate apps where needed:
Partner App (Technicians)
Quick Partner App (Dedicated operational flow)

👉 Result:

A plug-and-play architecture where new business modules can be added without affecting existing ones
